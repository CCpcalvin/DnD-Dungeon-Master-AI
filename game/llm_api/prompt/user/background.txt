Generate a compelling theme and reason for the player's presence in the tower.

1. Theme:
- Create a dark fantasy or gothic horror atmosphere
- The tower should feel ancient and mysterious
- Include elements of magic and danger

2. Player's Motivation:
- Why is the player drawn to the tower?
- What personal stakes do they have?
- What do they hope to find or achieve?

3. Tone & Style:
- Write in second person ("You")
- Keep it concise (2-3 paragraphs max)
- Create intrigue and mystery
- Leave some details open for discovery
